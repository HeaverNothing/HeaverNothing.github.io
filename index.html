Powerlifting

In April 2015 my brother convinced a skinny nerd 16 year old me to build a home gym, even though at the time I thought it was a waste of my money but I am glad he did. Back then I was barely able to squat my own bodyweight, since then I have been progressing steadily.
In September 2016 I did my first competition, since then I have competed at a national level and even been fortunate enough to represent Ireland as a Under 66kg Under 23 lifter at the world championship in Helsingborg Sweden in 2019. In 2021 after not competing in nearly 2 years I represented Ireland again. I was fortunate to comeback with a bronze medal and a national record for squatting 210kg.
It is great to see my constant training and commitment for the past 5 years has finally paid off with me reaching a bucket list goal of "Be successful at an international level at something". I am already looking forward to when I get the opportunity to represent my country again.
